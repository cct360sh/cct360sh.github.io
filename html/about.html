About

Bonjour! My name is Alexandre Salem Lamouri Gagne. It’s a fairly long name so most people just call me Alex. I’m a second-year student at the University of Toronto Mississauga currently pursuing a double major in both Computer Science and Communication, Culture & Information Technology.

More about myself. I am half Moroccan, half French, born and raised in Montreal. I am also fluent in French, English, and Spanish. I am passionate about music, whether it be listening to music, making music or attending music festivals. I enjoy traveling, learning new languages and discovering different cultures. I also consider myself a “sneakerhead” as I love to collect and wear various sneakers. Did I mention I love floral outfits?

Now that we’re acquainted, let’s talk more about my goals and aspirations. By doing a double major in CS and CCIT, my goal is to become a versatile developer, capable of creating both beautiful front-ends and sophisticated back-ends for applications or websites. I also aspire to be part of a team that will harness the power of technology to leave the world a better place than we found it since I believe that technology is a tool and its applications, like artificial intelligence, machine learning or cryptocurrencies can change lives.
